About

I'm a Contact Center Operations Leader with 15+ years of experience running high-volume teams in financial services — from consumer and military lending at MidCountry Bank to loss mitigation at JPMorgan Chase during the 2008 financial crisis.

My edge is the intersection of people, process, and technology. I've built automated underwriting tools that cut error rates by 67%, redesigned entire IVR systems, and led teams of 40+ while maintaining strict regulatory compliance across SCRA, CFPB, FDCPA, and REG B.

Since 2024, I've gone all in on independent creation. I taught myself to code — building and deploying Eternal Echoes, a full-stack SaaS app, from scratch. I learned video production using Final Cut Pro and Motion, launched a podcast (2 Make A Long Story Short), and handle all production, editing, and promo in-house. The through-line is the same: take an idea and make it real.
