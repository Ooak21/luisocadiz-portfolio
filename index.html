Who We Are

We Build Protocols.
Not Promises.

Innovative Blockchain Solutions is a Las Vegas-based Web3 development firm building decentralized financial infrastructure for real-world assets.

We believe the future of finance is programmable, transparent, and accessible to everyone. Every product we build runs on-chain — auditable by anyone, controlled by no one.

Our first protocol, IMC (Illusive Motor Concepts), brings peer-to-peer auto financing to the blockchain. Vehicle loans funded by real investors, settled in USDC, secured by NFT-backed collateral on Polygon.

Technology

Battle-Tested
Infrastructure

// 01
Polygon PoS
EVM-compatible Layer 2 with ~2 second finality and sub-cent transaction costs. MATIC secured proof-of-stake consensus.
// 02
Solidity Vaults
Custom smart contract vault architecture. Each loan is its own isolated contract — investor funds, yield distribution, and payment logic fully automated.
// 03
USDC Settlement
Circle's regulated USD-pegged stablecoin. Zero volatility exposure for investors. Payments settle on-chain within seconds.
// 04
ERC-721 NFTs
Vehicle assets are minted as NFTs on-chain. Each token stores make, model, VIN, appraisal value, and vault address permanently on Polygon.
// 05
MetaMask + WalletConnect
Wallet-native authentication. No passwords. No accounts. Connect your wallet, sign a message, access your position instantly from any device.
// 06
Supabase Backend
Military-grade PostgreSQL with row-level security. Every record encrypted at rest. Wallet-based authentication replaces passwords entirely — more secure than DocuSign, more transparent than any bank.
